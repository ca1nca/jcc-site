Dr. Viktorija Joga

Experiencing life to the fullest requires health and wellbeing. It is my mission as a Chiropractor to help individuals not only achieve optimal health, but to also embrace wellbeing, which requires a more holistic analysis of an individual’s emotional and physical health.

The body is complex, and each section of the body doesn’t function independently. It’s critical to consider not only the muscular-skeletal dynamics in the body, but also the external factors that might be affecting the body emotionally and environmentally. Chiropractic care encompasses careful analysis and diagnosis of the body, resulting in more effective customized treatment plans resulting in more successful results

Dr. Viktorija Joga graduated from Wittenberg University with a Bachelor of Arts in Biophysics and completed her studies in Radiation Therapy at Harvard Medical School. In 1989, she earned her Doctorate from the Palmer College of Chiropractic in Davenport, Iowa. During her time there, she served on staff at the college in the x-ray and diagnostics department. She is proficient in Rock Tape Kinesiotaping, Graston Therapy, myofascial and Active Release Technique, Activator, SOT, Thompson Drop and McKenzie protocol. Her emphasis is on proper diagnosis and customized patient care programs.

She presently resides in Redondo Beach with her husband and her two college age children and is active in the Rotary Club of Westchester, as well as a variety of organizations affiliated with her Lithuanian heritage. She actively pursues activities that give back to the communities through Service above Self.
