Common Work Injuries

The injuries that a person may face depends largely on the type of work that they do. Construction workers or those who have very physical jobs are more susceptible to overexertion injuries. These occur when lifting incorrectly, pushing and pulling heavy loads or throwing items. Slipping or tripping on wet floors or over objects on the ground is common in restaurant-type jobs.

 Other injuries include falling from heights, like ladders or roofs, walking-into injuries or getting hit by falling objects. In an office-type environment, many people suffer from repetitive motion injuries. These problems can be caused by excessive typing or heavy computer use and can cause issues like strained muscles and back or neck pain. Work or accident injuries may result in migraines, chronic back or neck aches, strains, sprains and more.
