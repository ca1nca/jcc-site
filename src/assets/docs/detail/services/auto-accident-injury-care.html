Types of Auto Accident Injuries Treated 

Injuries sustained from a car collision are not always visible to the naked eye. Internal injuries may be present, but may cause no pain or other symptoms. However, untreated injuries can lead to severe health complications. These are common auto accident injuries that we treat:

    • Concussions
    • Contusions
    • Whiplash
    • Neck and back injuries
    • Herniated or bulging discs
    • Vertebrae fracture 
    • Muscle strains, sprains, or tears 
    • Spinal misalignment 

Typical Symptoms after a Car Collision 

The following symptoms may develop right away or days or weeks later. For example, patients who suffered a whiplash may not notice symptoms until 24 hours or more after the initial trauma.

    • Neck pain or stiffness
    • Upper or lower back pain
    • Shoulder pain
    • Headaches or dizziness
    • Swelling and inflammation 
    • Sciatica (nerve pain)
    • Numbness or tingling in the arms or legs
    • Muscle pain or weakness

Diagnosing Your Pain and Injuries 

Our chiropractor will examine you thoroughly for head, neck, and back injuries. Non-obvious injuries to the spinal bones, joints, discs, and connective tissue may show up on an X-ray, CT scan, or MRI.
