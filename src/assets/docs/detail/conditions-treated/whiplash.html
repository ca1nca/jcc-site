Whiplash Injury Pain Management & Rehabilitation

A whiplash injury occurs when the force of a rear-impact accident “whips” the cervical spine beyond its normal range of motion, abruptly knocking the neck backwards and forwards. This whipping acting can knock the spine out of alignment, causing nerve compression and straining the muscles. Common symptoms of whiplash injury include neck pain, headaches at the base of the neck, soreness and stiffness in the neck, difficulty concentrating on simple tasks, and difficulty sleeping at night. While medication can relieve the immediate pain you may be experiencing, drugs are limited in their ability to deliver long-lasting pain relief. Once the medication wears off, your pain will return. That’s no way to live your life!

Unlike medication that only “covers up” pain symptoms, adjustments correct the underlying spinal misalignment that is compressing the nerves and causing your pain. Our gentle adjustments are safe and effective for individuals of all ages. Cervical spine adjustments correct the underlying cause for your pain – a cervical spine misalignment ­– by reducing nerve compression.
